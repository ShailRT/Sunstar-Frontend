who we are

Established in 2005, Sunstar Precision Forge Limited is a state-of-the-art manufacturing facility conceptualized and established to cater to the unprecedented demand for forged and machined components from automotive sector as well as defense, marine, oil and gas, and railways.

Sunstar is flexible for its customers. We place our customers at the centre of the organization and work according to their needs to deliver greatest value and satisfaction. We have adopted a long term relationship development approach and believe in continuous reinvestment in our facilities to offer endless solutions and services to our customers. This enables us to grow and develop with our customers.

Sunstar is a closed-die forging facility specializing in production of fully finished, forged, and machined components. We believe in providing complete product solutions to our customers, from design to manufacturing, value added services, testing and evaluation.We believe in dedicated teamwork, and encourage an open working environment of knowledge creation, learning, and innovation.
